Payday Apportioner

Please enter your expenses for the month.

  • Expense Name: Any name you would like to call your expense (e.g. capital one, car loan, electric/gas)
  • Monthly amount: How much do you pay per month? (this calculator accounts for two pay period a month; 24 total per year.)
  • Mandatory: Is part of all of the expense mandatory? (e.g. a credit card may have a $50 minimum payment, but you pay $100.)
  • Amount mandatory: How much of the expense is mandatory? (e.g. for a credit card, the minimum payment is mandatory; anything extra is not.)
